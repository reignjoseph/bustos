How It Works

Create an Account

Click the sign in button and then find the sign up to create an account. To create an account, you will need your Google account and password.

Fill out the registration form

as Job Seeker

Fill up all the information needed from step 1 to step 9. Make sure to input and choose the correct information needed.

as an Employer

Fill up the basic informations needed and upload the documents that the PESO required for the legitimacy of the business like BIR certificate of Registration, and any of the documents that depends on the employer type such as private, overseas, local agency, etc.

Find Job

After the registration, in order to find job that meets the applicants preference. There is an advanced search button wherein the applicant needs to answer like, the skill they have, their preferred locations, experience, salary and etc.

How Employers Post Job

Once the employers have completed their registration, they can now post the job vacancy. On the home page, there is a post button where employers need to fill out certain information, like company name, locations, job title, salary, quantity of the job vacant, and a detailed job description together with the requirements they need.

Apply Job

Once you find the job you want, simply click the apply button and send your resume and other application materials. Check on your application status for future interviews and other application progress.
